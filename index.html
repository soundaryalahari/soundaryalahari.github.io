About Me

Soundaryalahari is a young and talented dancer from Hyderabad. She has undergone rigorous training at Shankarananda Kalakshetra for 13 years under the able tutelage of her Guru Padmashri Dr. Ananda Shankar Jayant. From the tender age of 10, she has been a part of the institutes dance productions.

Tall and statuesque, with a lissome visage, along with a deep musical understanding, Soundaryalahari brings to her dance a quiet strength & sensitivity of portrayal, internalizing the deeper meaning of her art.

Lahari trains young students at Shankarananda kalakshetra and is also a budding choreographer who has been conferred with the NRITYAMANI award by the Cuttak Mahotsav. She is a graded artist in "Dance artist panel" in Doordarshan kendra, Hyderabad.


She has been a part of the ensemble of Shankarananda Kalakshetra and has had the privilege of participating in the many well-known productions of the institution, such as MohanaKrishna beckons, Dashavataram, and Dancing tales Panchatantra and has performed at important festivals.

Lahari has traveled extensively for her performances, which include both solo and group performances.She has performed in China & South Korea for the ICCR (2015), at BITS PILANI (Hyderabad,2013 & 2015), IIT's Cultural fest Sarang (Chennai, 2009), World Ikebana Conference (2007), 17th International Childrens Film Festival (Hyderabad, 2011), Nandi Film Award Function (2012), AP formation day at AP Bhavan (New Delhi, 2011).

Lahari is also an accomplished Carnatic Vocalist, having trained under Guru S.K.Venkatachary. She is currently also pursuing a course in Charted Accountancy from the ICAI.
